About

Our mission is to connect fans with unforgettable live music experiences and streamline your concert experience. Our platform brings together top concerts, festivals, and performances, making it easy to discover events and purchase tickets in just a few clicks.

Whether you're looking for global superstars, rising artists, or local live shows, we make sure you never miss the music you love.

Our Story

Amplify was created with a simple idea: live music should be easier to discover and more accessible for both fans and artists. Too often, incredible performances go unnoticed because they’re difficult to find or promoted on platforms that prioritize only the biggest names. We wanted to build a space where every performance—from global tours to local underground shows—could be discovered and celebrated. What started as a vision to simplify concert discovery quickly evolved into something bigger. Amplify is more than just a ticketing platform; it’s a community built around the energy and connection that live music creates. By bringing together fans, venues, and artists in one place, we help make the concert experience seamless from start to finish. For fans, Amplify makes it easy to explore upcoming shows, discover new artists, and secure tickets with just a few clicks. For artists, it provides a platform to promote performances, reach new audiences, and grow their presence within the live music scene. We believe live music is more than entertainment—it’s an experience that brings people together. Amplify exists to make those moments easier to find, easier to attend, and impossible to forget.
